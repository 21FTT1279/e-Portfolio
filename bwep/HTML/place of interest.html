Sultan Omar Ali Saifuddien Mosque

Brunei's most iconic picture is the Omar Ali Saifuddien Mosque in Bandar Seri Begawan.
The mosque, which overlooks the Brunei River and Kampong Ayer, is surrounded by an artificial lagoon/reflecting pool.
This is one of Brunei's most photogenic sights.

Kampong Ayer

Kampong Ayer is consistently ranked as one of Brunei's finest tourist destinations, offering an experience unlike any other.
A maze-like network of wooden boardwalks connects more than 40 minor communities in the medieval floating village.
Kampong Ayer is home to 13,000 people who have their own schools, mosques, and stores.

Tutong town

Tutong Town, nestled between the city's two busiest areas, is the ideal pitstop for those searching for a change of pace.
Stop over for a glimpse of the Tutong River and have some coffee time at one of the 'kopitiam' restaurant.

Pantai Seri Kenangan

The Tutong Town Centre is around five minutes away.
A short spit of land separates the mirror-smooth Tutong river from the ruffled seas of the South China Sea, giving the beach its allure.

Belalong Canopy Walkway

Cross five interconnected towers with a canopy height of more than 43 meters.
From up here, you can get a bird's eye perspective of Brunei's rainforests, which are home to one of the world's most diversified ecosystems.

Sultan Haji Omar Ali Saifuddien Bridge

Sultan Haji Omar 'Ali Saifuddien Bridge, which stretches over 30 kilometers, is one of Southeast Asia's longest bridges.
The Temburong neighborhood was connected to the mainland via a dual-carriageway bridge that opened in March 2020.

Teraja Waterfalls

The Teraja Waterfalls encourage tourists to explore its beautiful pools and meander through its lush surroundings,
which are home to a diverse range of flora and fauna.
With over 40 waterfalls in Labi's natural landscape, you'll want to take a tour to get the whole experience!

Anduki Recreational Park

The Anduki Recreational Park, was opened in 1992 to commemorate Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah's silver jubilee,
is the ideal hideaway for locals and visitors alike.
Set against a stunning backdrop of a shimmering skyblue lake, the park is a tranquil setting for an afternoon get-together,
or add some excitement to your day with some radio-controlled boat racing.
