Tutong




      

Tutong Town

Tutong Town, nestled between the city's two busiest areas, is the ideal pitstop for those searching for a change of pace.
Stop over for a glimpse of the Tutong River and have some coffee time at one of the 'kopitiam' restaurant.



Pantai Seri Kenangan

The Tutong Town Centre is around five minutes away.
A short spit of land separates the mirror-smooth Tutong river from the ruffled seas of the South China Sea, giving the beach its allure.



Lamin Warisan

This historic location has been transformed into a museum dedicated to preserving Tutong's culture and ethnic history.
Visitors may learn about the history of Tanjung Maya township and Tutong's past by looking through antique images and carefully preserved artefacts.



Tasbee Meliponiculture Farm

Over a hundred nests of stingless bees are housed at Tasbee Meliponiculture Farm for the exquisite "Kelulut Honey."
Aside from their sweet gold, they have honey soaps, candles, and other honey-based goods that are all created locally from their own bee colonies.



Tasek Merimbun

Tasek Merimbun is Brunei Darussalam's largest natural lake and has been declared as one of ASEAN's Heritage Parks.
Its vast range of flora and fauna, as well as its diversified range of species, make for a thrilling journey for intrepid wildlife aficionados.
