Kuala Belait




       




Teraja Waterfalls

The Teraja Waterfalls encourage tourists to explore its beautiful pools and meander through its lush surroundings,
which are home to a diverse range of flora and fauna.
With over 40 waterfalls in Labi's natural landscape, you'll want to take a tour to get the whole experience!



Anduki Recreational Park

The Anduki Recreational Park, was opened in 1992 to commemorate Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah's silver jubilee,
is the ideal hideaway for locals and visitors alike.
Set against a stunning backdrop of a shimmering skyblue lake, the park is a tranquil setting for an afternoon get-together,
or add some excitement to your day with some radio-controlled boat racing.



Billionth Barrel Monument

The Billionth Barrel Monument, which stands along the seashore in Seria and was built in 1991, is a monument of Brunei's oil production success.
The landmark edifice, which bears the name of the achievement, stands tall and proud for everyone to see.
This landmark is located on the beach in Seria, which features stunning sunsets and distant views of oil rigs.



Mendaram Longhouses

Mendaram Besar Longhouse, with its unique red roofs and 12-doors, was erected in the 1970s and today houses roughly 100 people.
The home was made of palm leaves back then.
The mansion has undergone several renovations throughout the years, including the installation of hardwood planks, contemporary stairs, and corrugated roofs.



Seria Energy Lab

Check out the Seria Energy Lab, a play and learning center for kids!
The lab was created to pique the interest of young brains, and it houses a number of exhibits centered on energy.
Make sure to check out the AquaLab, a 70,000-litre cylindrical aquarium tank housed within a jacket leg that houses a variety of fish and aquatic species.
